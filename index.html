About Me

My career has taken me through diverse roles, from operating heavy machinery to assembly line work, culminating in my current position at Ventil Test Equipment BV.

Initially hired as a Service Engineer, my role has organically evolved towards Research & Development. I've been deeply involved in writing in-depth systems analyses, proposing data-driven frameworks for Quality Assurance improvement, and developing operational manuals for key machinery. I am passionate about systems thinking, problem-solving, and contributing to innovative engineering solutions.

My goal is to leverage my analytical skills and hands-on experience to transition into a dedicated Systems Engineering role, ideally within an innovative sector like the space industry. I am highly adaptable, a quick learner, and thrive in environments where I can contribute to meaningful technological advancements.

Key Projects & Contributions

Pekos Clamping System: Engineering Analysis

Conducted an in-depth engineering analysis to diagnose and resolve critical operational inconsistencies in the Pekos clamping system. Identified root causes related to gain margin issues and mechanical variances, leading to a data-driven optimization of force parameters and control logic.

Impact: Improved force consistency by up to 350% on affected stations, restoring reliable operation and ensuring quality assurance for critical components.

Framework for Enhanced Performance Validation

Authored a comprehensive proposal for a standardized framework to enhance performance validation and analysis of Ventil clamping systems. This framework introduces a tiered approach with objective metrics and acceptance criteria for data-driven QA.

Impact: Aims to improve product quality, strengthen QA evidence, enable efficient troubleshooting, and build a knowledge base for continuous improvement, supporting Ventil's strategic goals.

View Framework (PDF)

VCB-4S Operational Manual Development

Developed a detailed operational manual for the VCB-4S machine line, one of Ventil's primary products. This involved understanding the machine's functionality, safety procedures, maintenance, and troubleshooting.

Impact: Provides clear, concise, and accurate documentation for operators, enhancing safety, operational efficiency, and user understanding of the equipment.
